Who We Are

About Maxwell Meridian

A Singapore-based engineering consultancy built on technical rigour and trusted delivery.

Engineering with Purpose

Maxwell Meridian Engineering Pte Ltd is a specialist M&E engineering consultancy headquartered in Singapore. We serve architects, developers, main contractors, and government agencies with professional, code-compliant, and cost-effective building services solutions.

Our team comprises registered Professional Engineers, senior design engineers, and BIM modellers with deep cross-sector experience. We approach every project with a focus on buildability, long-term maintainability, and regulatory compliance from the outset.

We believe good engineering is invisible — systems that work reliably, efficiently, and quietly in the background, enabling the spaces they serve.

Why Clients Choose Us

  • Registered Professional Engineers (PE Board)
  • BCA Green Mark accredited designs
  • Full BIM capability (Revit, Navisworks, BIM 360)
  • In-house authority submission experience
  • Proven track record on complex mixed-use projects
  • Long-term client relationships — 70% repeat business
  • Responsive, senior-led project teams
